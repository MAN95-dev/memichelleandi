A little bit more about me

I'm a passionate Front End Developer with a diverse background. I graduated from the University of Cape Town with a degree in English and Psychology before moving to China to teach English. There, I took on a management role and helped develop and grow our team. When the Covid pandemic hit, I decided to pivot my career and pursue web development. I obtained a diploma in full-stack development and then made the move to the UK, where I am now based.

As a Front End Developer, I specialize in UX/UI design, which includes conducting research, designing wireframes and mockups, and creating prototypes for each project. I take pride in managing the projects within our IT department, ensuring that timelines are followed and projects are delivered to the specification required. With my varied background and passion for learning new skills, I'm always eager to tackle new challenges and push the boundaries of what's possible in web development.
