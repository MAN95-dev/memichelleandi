Hammertime Fulfilment

Hammertime Fulfilment is a company that offers fulfilment, warehousing, and distribution services to businesses. They provide storage space for goods, along with fulfilment services to manage the distribution of those goods. Additionally, they offer the convenience of onsite meeting facilities and tours of their facilities to clients. This can help businesses streamline their logistics and provide a seamless customer experience.


I sat with key stakeholders to gather project requirements and understand their business. Then conducted market research to analyse competitors' offerings and identify potential opportunities. Based on these findings, built designs that met the needs and expectations of the stakeholders. This ensured that the project was aligned with business objectives and provided a user-friendly interface.

The Process

The Problem

A common problem when searching for warehousing storage, fulfilment, and distribution services is a lack of information about the services and the company offering them. This can lead to uncertainty about whether the company can meet your specific needs and expectations.

The Goal

To develop a website that provides potential clients with information about the warehousing storage, fulfilment, and distribution services available while concurrently highlighting the company's core values and organisational culture.

Why?

Without clear information about the services offered, it can be difficult to determine if the company provides the type of warehousing storage, fulfilment, and distribution services required for your business. This lack of clarity can also make it challenging to compare services and prices with those offered by other providers in the industry.

In addition to this, not knowing anything about the company can create doubts about the quality and reliability of their services. It is essential to have an understanding of the company's work culture, values, and experience to ensure that they can meet your business needs and expectations.

Overall, a lack of information about the services and the company can create significant challenges when searching for warehousing storage, fulfilment, and distribution services. It is important to conduct thorough research and seek out detailed information to ensure that you select a provider that is best suited to your business needs.
